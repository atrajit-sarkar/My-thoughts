**Can you handle infinity? Below is a example how to handle:**
$$
\sum_{m>0}\frac{1}{m^3}=\sum_{m_1>m_2>0}\frac{1}{m_1^2m_2}
$$
or in other words,
$$\zeta(3)=\zeta(2,1)$$
